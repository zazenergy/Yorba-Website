Straighten photos

The straighten tools allows photos to be leveled and straightened. The straighten tool is only available in full-window or fullscreen mode.

  1. Click Straighten. The straighten slider will appear. Alternatively, use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+A.

  2. Drag the slider to achieve the desired degree of straightening.

  3. Press OK when finished.
