Donating to Yorba

If you like our projects and would like to help support our software development efforts, consider making a financial contribution to the Yorba Foundation. We accept donations via PayPal to donate@yorba.org.

The Yorba Foundation is a non-profit California corporation and has applied for tax-exempt 501(c)(3) status, but has not yet received a tax exemption ruling from the IRS. We'll email all donors with an update once we receive a ruling about our status.

Thanks for your support!
