Together, we're building great software.
Here's how you can help:

Translation

Shotwell has been translated into over 50 languages thanks to our dedicated community of volunteer translators. If you'd like to help translate Shotwell into your language, check out our project page on Transifex. To translate other projects, please contact info@yorba.org.

Testing

We're on a mission to squash bugs! Before filing a bug, please check whether it's already been reported at Yorba Redmine. If not, please create an account on Redmine and file a bug. Learn how to create the perfect bug report here.



Development

Yorba welcomes volunteers who want to contribute code to Shotwell and Geary. All our projects are coded in Vala. Please visit our wiki to get started. You'll want to read our coding conventions too.

Interested in working on any of our currently inactive projects? Shoot us an email at info@yorba.org and we'll help you get started.

Plugins

You can write plugins that extend Shotwell with new publishing destinations, slideshow effects and more. Read our plugin documentation to learn more.
