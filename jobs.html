Working at Yorba

We're looking for exceptional software engineers interested in joining our team to work on Shotwell, Geary or other upcoming Yorba projects.

We can offer you

  • The opportunity to work on cutting-edge multimedia software using cool open source technologies such as GNOME, GTK+ and Vala.
  • A work location in San Francisco's Mission District, easily accessible by public transportation and surrounded by zillions of restaurants and cool nightlife.
  • A great team to work with: we are friendly and enthusiastic and have years of open source experience.
  • Flexible vacation (six weeks per year including holidays) and health insurance benefits.

Here's what we're looking for:

  • You have a computer science degree or equivalent expertise gained through work experience.
  • You can write C code in your sleep. You are also very proficient in at least one object-oriented language such as Java or C#.
  • You can jump into a project with many thousands of lines of code and be productive within days.
  • You like usable graphical interfaces.
  • You like to think about design decisions - both about user interface decisions and coding/architectural decisions.
  • You have permission to work in the U.S. and can work with us at least four days per week in our office in San Francisco.
  • You're excited about open source software and about changing the world.

    • If this sounds like you, we'd love to hear from you! Send your resume to jobs@yorba.org.
